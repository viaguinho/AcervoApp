================================================================================
STITCH IMAGE PROMPTS - ACERVO APP DESIGN UPGRADE
================================================================================

Este arquivo contém os prompts recomendados e formatados para uso no Google Stitch
para gerar os elementos gráficos atualizados do Acervo App.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PARTE 1: IMAGEM DE BOAS-VINDAS & ONBOARDING (WELCOME / LANDING PAGE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Use este prompt para criar imagens abstratas, com texturas contemporâneas e desfoques
artísticos para servir de fundo e conceito na tela inicial do aplicativo.

PROMPT:
[Concept & Mood]
Create a high-end, abstract, and contemporary conceptual image representing the refined essence of fine mixology, private bar collections, and slow-sipping spirits. The aesthetic should be highly artistic, dramatic, and luxurious, avoiding literal bar counters or glass shapes. 

[Visual Elements & Composition]
- Subject: An abstract interplay of fluid dynamics and glass textures. Think of slow-motion liquid glass drops suspended in mid-air, casting beautiful, long refractions.
- Texture & Effects: Rich, organic shapes resembling liquid amber, caramel gold, and dark graphite oils blending together. Introduce a sophisticated, dreamlike cinematic blur (deep bokeh) and motion sweep that implies slow, luxurious movement (like a fluid macro shot).
- Lighting: Atmospheric and low-key. A single directional beam of warm amber light cutting through a dark, hazy, smoky charcoal studio background, catching the edges of transparent textures and fluid contours.
- Tone: Premium, editorial, and mysterious. The artwork must feel like a modern luxury brand campaign (similar to high-fashion perfume or luxury watch backdrops).

[Color Palette]
Deep piano black, graphite grey, warm honey-amber, and glowing copper lights. 

[Photography Specs]
Photorealistic, macro lens, f/1.8 for extreme shallow depth of field, high contrast, cinematic atmosphere, 8k resolution.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PARTE 2: CATÁLOGO DE GARRAFAS (SUBSTITUIÇÃO DE FUNDO / AMBIENTAÇÃO DE PRODUTO)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Use este prompt juntamente com as imagens das garrafas recortadas em formato PNG
para integrá-las aos cenários fotográficos premium de acordo com cada categoria.

PROMPT:
[Role & Context]
You are a world-class commercial beverage photographer and high-end digital composition artist specializing in luxury spirits, mixology cataloging, and editorial bar applications. 

[Input Image Treatment]
Isolate the bottle from the input image. Do NOT alter the bottle shape, font face, brand logo, label text, or physical glass structure of the bottle. Keep all reflections on the glass and the original color of the liquid intact. 

[Composition & Environment Goal]
Create a premium, realistic studio product shot. Place the bottle standing naturally on a solid, upscale surface that matches its category, surrounded by a minimalist, color-harmonized, and atmospheric background. The lighting must be cinematic, featuring dramatic softbox side lights and a subtle back-glow highlighting the bottle’s silhouette and the liquid within.

[Style Categories & Custom Backgrounds]

1. AMARO / APERITIVO (e.g., Ramazzotti, Averna, Lucano, Fernet)
   - Color Palette: Deep amber, warm mahogany, dark graphite, and burnt orange hues.
   - Surface: Polished dark walnut wood or honed dark slate with wet/dew micro-reflections.
   - Background: Dark, moody, softly blurred bar alcove with warm backlighting passing through amber glass elements, creating a subtle, luxurious bokeh effect.

2. CACHAÇA (e.g., Aviador, Sebastiana)
   - Color Palette: Warm gold, soft straw yellow, clean whites, and raw green accents.
   - Surface: Textured Brazilian freijó wood or a brushed light-gray concrete slab.
   - Background: Minimalist studio gradient blending clean white-ice with warm golden sun rays filtering through abstract tropical palm leaf silhouettes in the distance (soft defocus).

3. MEZCAL / TEQUILA (e.g., 400 Conejos, Ojo de Tigre, Patrón)
   - Color Palette: Charcoal gray, volcanic black, terracotta, and soft agave green.
   - Surface: Rough-hewn volcanic basalt stone or raw Mexican travertine.
   - Background: A modern architectural backdrop with raw concrete textures, subtle shadows of agave plants, and dramatic light beams slicing through a dusty, dark studio atmosphere.

4. WHISKY (e.g., Woodford Reserve, Talisker, Suntory Toki, Monkey Shoulder)
   - Color Palette: Deep golden honey, copper, peat-smoke gray, and brushed metallic silver.
   - Surface: Distressed dark oak cask stave or polished black Nero Marquina marble.
   - Background: An elegant, warm study environment with dark, textured plaster walls, faint copper distillery pipe details in the soft-focus background, and warm light highlighting the liquid's crystalline texture.

5. RUM (e.g., Appleton Estate, Bacardi)
   - Color Palette: Rich molasses brown, copper, dark sand, and deep navy blue.
   - Surface: Weathered teak wood or dark-grained volcanic sand.
   - Background: High-contrast studio setup with a dark, moody background featuring subtle tropical palm leaf reflections and rich copper-accented lighting.

6. LICOR (e.g., Chambord, Bottega Raspberry/Pistacchio, Baileys)
   - Color Palette: Harmonized with the liqueur's flavor profile (e.g., rich raspberry magenta, pistachio green, creamy beige, or deep cocoa).
   - Surface: Seamless matte marble matching the drink's primary tone.
   - Background: A luxurious, color-matched abstract studio backdrop with satin fabric folds, soft velvet textures, and high-fashion editorial lighting.

[Photography Specs]
- Camera: Shot on 85mm prime lens, f/4.0 for sharp bottle details and a buttery, soft-focused background.
- Lighting: Professional studio lighting. Rim lighting to separate the bottle from the background. Diffused key light to avoid harsh highlights on the labels.
- Output: Photorealistic, ultra-high detail (8k), commercial advertising quality, no watermark, no text overlays, clean composition.
